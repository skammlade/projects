YGR Horsetooth Time Trial Series

sara

31 March 2016



Fort Collins, CO is home to a thriving grassroots racing scene. Your Group Ride hosts an annual time trial every Tuesday in April known as the Horsetooth Time Trial Series. The hilly 14.35 mile course runs along Horsetooth Reservoire and Horsetooth Mountain Park climbing a total of 1,336 feet.




I wanted to see how the racer turnout compared between men versus women. 2015 was the best turnout for female cyclsts with 34 total races.
