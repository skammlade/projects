YGR Horsetooth Time Trial Series

sara

31 March 2016




Fort Collins, CO is home to a thriving grassroots racing scene. Your Group Ride hosts an annual time trial every Tuesday in April known as the Horsetooth Time Trial Series. The hilly 14.35 mile course runs along Horsetooth Reservoire and Horsetooth Mountain Park climbing a total of 1,336 feet.






I wanted to see how the racer turnout compared between men versus women so I plotted race data from 2011-2015. Last year had the greatest turnout for female cyclsts so far with 34 racers representing 21.25% of total racers.




I thought it was a little strange that 2013 had such a low showing. I pulled some temperature data from WeatherUnderground for each date during the race time and found it was rideable weather for all of the races!
